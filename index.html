Chicken Biryani

This chicken biryani recipe features succulent, juicy pieces of chicken that are cooked in a yogurt marinade and then layered with crispy onions, coriander, mint and basmati rice to give you a dish that the entire family will enjoy.

Ingredients:

Chicken 100 grams
Tomato 4 medium size
Onion 1 medium size
Oil 2tb
Spices Shan chicken biryani masala
Yougurt Half cup

Total

60 mints
